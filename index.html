This chart and the next is based on a dummy data set of teacher assessments.

All District Ratings

Each column shows the percent composition of rating categories (highly ineffective through highly effective) on one assessment (TP, SP State, SP Local). For example, District 6 had the following breakdown on the TP: 40% Developing, 47% Effective, and 13% Highly Effective.

District 6 Points

District 6 Average Overall Points: 81.7

Percent of District 6 Teachers with Overall Rating of Effective or higher: 100%

Each stack in a column represents the results of an assessment: the height corresponds to the point value, while the color represents the rating. The bottom, middle, and top stacks represent the points from the TP, SP State, and SP Local assessments respectively. The total height of a column represents the Overall Points value for an individual teacher. For example, Durian received 49 points and a rating of Developing on the TP (bottom stack) and 15 points and a rating of Effective on both the SP State and the SP Local (middle and top stacks) for a total of 79 Overall Points.
